Who We Are

At INTARC Interiors, we are a dedicated team of skilled designers and architects based in Trivandrum, Kollam and Kerala. With a passion for creating beautiful and functional spaces, we specialize in providing comprehensive interior design solutions.

Our expertise spans from conceptualization to completion, ensuring every project reflects our commitment to quality and client satisfaction. Let us transform your space with elegance, innovation, and precision.
